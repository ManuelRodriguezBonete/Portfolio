Battle - Ween

Available on Web browser for Pc.
Developed in Phaser5.

Battle-Ween is a battleroyale, multiplayer videogame where the player can choose between some Halloween characters.
You can play with a friend in the same computer and the same keyboard.
Every match in Battle-Ween has 5 maps, the first player that wins 3 of them, wins the match.
