B O BB

Available on mobile devices (Android).
Developed in Unity.

Bobb is a singleplayer videogame where the player controlls Bobb, one little ball whose objective is move along his world with out dying.
You can move Bobb with your mobile's gyroscope.
If Bobb touches one spike or one saw, the level will be reseted.
