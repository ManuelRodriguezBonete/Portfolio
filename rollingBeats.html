GDD Rolling Beats

Rolling beats is a rhythm video game where you play as Tina Rules, a skater whose dream is to become the first person to skate on the rings of Saturn.
Each level in Rolling Beats has a duration of 2 minutes with an unique song.
In Rolling Beats the player will find two carousels, an upper one and a lower one where the notes will arrive, which they will have to press at the right time to get more points and increase the combo multiplier.
